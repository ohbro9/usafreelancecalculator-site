About The Product

One toolkit for freelance rates, fees, tax, goals, and budget planning.

USA Freelance Calculator Toolkit is built for freelancers who want practical money planning tools without sign-up friction. Each tool works on its own, and the connected workflow lets you move from pricing to fees, taxes, income goals, and final budgeting without re-entering the same context every time.

Built For
US freelancers, contractors, and independent service businesses
How It Works
Standalone tools + connected handoff between the 5 calculators
Main Goal
Fast planning, cleaner decisions, and premium no-signup usability

What the toolkit covers

  • Hourly Rate for turning time and income targets into billable pricing.
  • Platform Fees for seeing what you keep before tax after marketplace and payout fees.
  • Tax Estimator for federal and self-employment planning estimates.
  • Income Goal Planner for translating goals into required rates and workload assumptions.
  • Budget Planner for runway, reserve, and expense planning.

Standalone or connected

You can use each tool directly from its own page, or move through the connected flow from one tool to the next. Connected handoff is designed for convenience, not lock-in. If you only need one calculator, use that calculator.

The current connected order is Hourly Rate → Platform Fees → Tax Estimator → Income Goal Planner → Budget Planner.

Product philosophy

The toolkit is designed to feel like a focused financial product, not a pile of disconnected forms. The goal is fast clarity, premium presentation, clean exports, and browser-local convenience for freelancers who want planning help without forcing accounts or dashboards first.
